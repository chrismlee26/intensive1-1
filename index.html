who are we?

We enable makers by facilitating safer pre-purchasing. when the number of orders meets the number required the whole thing begins.

What is Limited Run?

‘Limited Run’: A new platform enabling demand based batch manufacturing and pre-purchasing of specialty goods. It’s a combination of a group-buy mechanism, crowdfunding platform, and project management, communication software and payment processor.

  • For Makers

    ◦ Guarantee your orders before you make.

    ◦ Tools to help you plan your project, calculate profit and establish milestones.

    ◦ Communicate with your buyers automatically.

    ◦ Payment processing.

    ◦ Market to your community your way. Use our platform and/or API Integration for your own website.


  • For Buyers

    ◦ Specialty items made just for you.

    ◦ Safer pre-purchasing through our escrow.

    ◦ Forced communication and updates from Makers.

    ◦ We vet makers for you through our internal algorithms and show this in a ‘safe maker’ rating.

    ◦ Don’t pay until manufacturing starts.

    ◦ Lower prices than traditional retail.


  • How it works

    ◦ Plan your project.

    ◦ Calculate profit.

    ◦ Establish milestones.

    ◦ Market to your community.

    ◦ Upload photos as you go and communicate with buyers.

    ◦ Payments released based on your schedule.

    ◦ Automated delivery when you're ready.
