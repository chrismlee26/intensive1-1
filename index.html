who are we?

We enable makers by facilitating safer pre-purchasing. when the number of orders meets the number required the whole thing begins.

  • project management

    Tools to help you plan your project, calculate profit and establish milestones.

  • communication

    Automatically communicate with your buyers while making so they know what to expect.

  • escrow

    Budget release schedule based on project milestones so everyone stays protected.

"It's a group-buy mechanism for pre-ordering: A crowdfunding platform, project management software, communications hub with an escrow and payment service all in one."

for makers

Guarantee your order before you make. Payment processing. Communicate with your buyers. Project Management software works for you. Market to your community your way. Use our platform and/or API Integration for your own website.

for buyers

Safer pre-purchasing through our escrow. Enforced communication and updates from Makers. We vet makers for you through our internal algorithms and show this in a ‘safe maker’ rating. Don’t pay until manufacturing starts. Lower prices than traditional retail.
